History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Co. began as a small outdoor adventure company offering guided rafting tours on the Colorado River. With a passion for adventure and sustainability, the company quickly grew, expanding to other major rivers in the U.S., including the Snake and the Arkansas River. White Water Rafting Co. pioneered eco-friendly rafting practices, focusing on preserving natural habitats while offering thrilling experiences. Over the years, the company expanded globally, now offering international tours in Costa Rica, New Zealand, and beyond, cementing its reputation as a leader in adventure tourism..
